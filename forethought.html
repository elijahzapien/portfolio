4Thought
(work in progress)

A resource for survival related products and information.

Disasters can occur at any time, and our preparedness to deal with those situations directly effects their outcome.

To emphasize these overwhelming scenarios, multiple themes were created depicting scenes of both mother nature, and human factors. A theme is loaded at random each time the user restarts their browser, or they can select a theme from the intro navigation.

"Prepare for the worst, plan for the unexpected, survive anything."
